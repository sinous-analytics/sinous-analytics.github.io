Quality, Analytics, and Innovations 2020

The Fifth Sino-US Research Conference on Quality, Analytics and Innovations

June 5-7, 2020, Wenzhou, China

 


Quality has become one of major themes for modern industries to achieve competitive advantages in the 21st century global market competition. The emphasis on Quality in industrial applications is being leveraged to a level higher than ever, thus giving researchers more opportunities and challenges. Innovation is the best solution to tackle raising challenges. Rapid advancements of sensing and communication technology promote the new development of Analytics to link Quality and Innovations, and enable the next-generation practices in industry and innovations in academic research.

This conference aims to bring researchers from US and China together, create a platform for sharing cutting-edge research fruits and promoting scholarly collaborations. This Conference constitutes a forum for engineers and scientists to exchange and disseminate knowledge and discoveries in quality, analytics and innovations, and therefore increases the broader impact on economic development of both countries.

Following the success of the 1st conference in 2016 (Shanghai), 2nd conference in 2017 (Beijing), 3rd conference in 2018 (Xi'An), 4th conference in 2019 (Xuzhou), the 5th Sino-US Research Conference on Quality, Analytics and Innovations will be held in Wenzhou, China June 5-7, 2020.

CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS

We call for submissions for oral and poster presentations on a wide range of topics about quality and reliability engineering, system informatics, data analytics, and design innovations. Relevant topics include (but are not limited to):

Quality and reliability engineering
Design and analyis of experiments
System informatics and information theory
Data science and statistics
Sensor-based modeling and analysis

Predictive and prescriptive analytics
Artificial intelligence and quality
Distributed systems and process control
Healthcare research and quality
Network science and graph theory

Data-driven modeling and innovations
Smart systems design
Simulation and optimization
Industrial operations management
Industrial Engineering education

Submissions should be made by emailing an extended abstract as a single PDF file to  sinous-analytics@outlook.com with the email subject line “Abstract Submission: YourName + University” prior to the deadline of abstract submission.
